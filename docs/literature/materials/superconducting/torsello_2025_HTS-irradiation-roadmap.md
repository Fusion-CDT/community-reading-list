Contemporary, dense, and star-studded paper that sheds light on the entire spectrum of issues faced by HTS materials for fusion, including: technology and manufacturing; materials modelling, and; experimentation and characterisation. A great starting point for any literature review, though it does make you feel a bit down in the dumps afterwards.
