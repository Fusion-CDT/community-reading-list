# EPFL Plasma Physics Online Lecture Course

This online lecture course servers as an introduction to plasma physics, starting from the kinetic and working through fluid descriptions of Plasma to MHD. The second part of this course, Plasma Phyiscs: Applications covers space plasma, low temperature plasma, and plasma for fusion in both MCF and ICF contexts.

Plasma Physics Introduction: https://courseware.epfl.ch/courses/course-v1:EPFL+PlasmaIntroduction+1T_2018/about
Plasma Physics Applications: https://courseware.epfl.ch/courses/course-v1:EPFL+PlasmaApplication+1T_2018/about

This is a complete lecture course including online lectures, Problems with example solutions and lecture notes.

This course was initially recommended to me by Dr Marco Cecconello as preparation of the fusion CDT, it is recommended to student who prefer a visual lecture as opposed to simply written notes, however the written notes for this course are unfortunately of poorer quality than the Oxford's MMathPhys course on Collisional Plasma Physics. In addition these notes are not quite as extensive as the Oxford notes on the theory side, not covering any drift kinetics, however they go into far more practical detail in the second part of the course.

Whilst not all parts of the applications section of this course will be relevant to all student, I would still highly recommend the space plasma sections as it is one of the best showcases for the scientific method that I have seen
