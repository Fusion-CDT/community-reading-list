# Lectures on Kinetic Theory and Magnetohydrodynamics of Plasmas

https://www-thphys.physics.ox.ac.uk/people/AlexanderSchekochihin/KT/2015/KTLectureNotes.pdf

The complete lectures notes by Alexander A.Schekochihin as part of the MMathPhys/MScMTP program at Oxford. These notes are thorough and extend from the initial kinetic description of plasma through to Kinetic MHD and Drift Kinetics. 

Whilst there are a number of good resources on MHD and kinetic theory available, I recommend these notes for a few very specific reasons

1.  These notes accompany the Oxford plasma physics lectures, which CDT student _may_ be able to attend since they are partially taught by UKAEA staff (ask nicely)
2. A very clear introduction to drift kinetics, and often overlook section of plasma physics
3. This material is a pre-requisite to Oxford's course on Gyrokinetics, which again, may be of specific interest to certain students
